Target Audience and Scenarios

This section describes the target audience for the Chamber of commerce website.

  • Who:The target audience are people (male and female) between 12-90 years who will easily access the website to check the business information.
  • Age:The target audience are people (male and female) between 12-90 years, who are seeking any kind of information about business
  • Education: High School, university graduates and professionals ie all business orders.
  • Technology: Visitors can visit our site via mobile devices, laptop and tablets to access any information about Businesses in the city of Owerri.
  • Motivation: Learn more about the Businesses in Owerri and succeed
