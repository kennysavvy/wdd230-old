SITE PURPOSE

The Owerri Chamber of Commerce and Industry was established for the promotion and protection of Trade and Industry and to represent and express the opinion of the business community on matters affecting trade and industry in the city of Owerri, the Owerri Port Complex, Imo State and where applicable, the whole Federation. It is a non-profit making organization and its income and property are applied solely for the promotion of its objectives.
